About · Patrick Smith

I am a digital product designer and developer from Melbourne, Australia, focused on creating apps for the web & Mac for passionate professionals, both as a freelancer and with my own projects.

Completing a Bachelor of Multimedia Design in 2009, I was a freelancer in web development and Mac app development, and founder of adventure sports video curator and creator InfinityList.com in 2011. I currently work at Coder Factory as a lead trainer.
